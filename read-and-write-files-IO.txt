Regardless of the source or destination of the data, the process of reading (input) and writing (output) data is the same:

- Reading (read)
1. Open a Stream
2. while(there is_information)
2.1 read_information
3. Close the Stream

- Writing (write)
1. Open a Stream
2. while(there is_information)
2.1 write_information
3. Close the Stream
